§01 / Problems we solve

When work is scattered, growth becomes harder to manage.

Arzware focuses on operational pain that small teams feel every day: missed leads, repeated follow-ups, unclear ownership, manual reporting and disconnected tools.

01

Missed leads

Potential customers disappear across WhatsApp, DMs, forms and calls without a single tracking view.

02

Manual follow-ups

Owners and teams rely on memory instead of reminders, statuses and clear next actions.

03

Spreadsheet chaos

Sheets become fragile mini-databases with duplicated rows, missing context and unclear ownership.

04

No dashboard visibility

No simple way to see leads, tasks, orders, requests or weekly performance at a glance.

05

Disconnected tools

Your website, booking, inbox, forms and team workflow do not talk to each other.

06

Slow reporting

Progress updates are rebuilt manually instead of generated from the actual workflow.

§04 / How it works

Diagnose first. Build only what the workflow needs.

The process is designed to reduce risk: map the work, prototype the system, launch a usable first version, then improve based on real usage.

Diagnose

Map the real workflow.

Understand customers, tools, handoffs, follow-ups, bottlenecks and missed opportunities.

Prototype

Make the system visible.

Sketch dashboards, flows, forms and data structure before committing to a bigger build.

Build

Launch the smallest useful system.

Create the website, dashboard, CRM-lite tracker, automation or workflow assistant needed first.

Launch & improve

Support real adoption.

Handover, document, review usage and decide what should improve after the first version works.
